About the event

Bouhjar International Chess Academy BICA a FIDE endorsed chess Academy under the auspices of Tunisian Chess Federation (TCF) is pleased to invite you to participate in the 1st BICA International chess tournament, which will be held at Iberostar Kantaoui Sousse {*****} and Sentido Bellevue Park {*****}, from 23th December to 31th, 2021.

Our Guests

Amir Zaibi (born 22 May 1988) is a Tunisian chess Grandmaster since 2020. He ranks 1st active chess player in Tunisia (International Chess Federation.) He won the International Arab Championship in Mostaganem in 2019 and obtained the title of Grandmaster on 4 December 2019.
Abdelrahman Hesham (born 1992) is an Egyptian chess player. He was awarded the title Grandmaster by FIDE in 2016. Hesham won the 2016 African Chess Championship, earning him the title of Grandmaster. He has also represented Egypt in two Chess Olympiads. In 2016, he played on board 5, ending on 5.5/9.In 2018, he played on board 4, finishing on 4.5/9.
